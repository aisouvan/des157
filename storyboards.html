Storyboards

For both of these storyboards, my partner and I tried to depict the storyline of a person with a mental illness choosing to use our website or app in order to feel better when in a tough situation. For example, an individual could be feeling depressed, saddened, anxious, or any other possible mental illness and choose to log and record how they are feeling when they are feeling it. This website we aim to help those individuals who may not want to or feel comfortable talking to a therapist or other professional. On this website they can also keep their personal issues in confidence while also having an outlet to control their feelings and thoughts.
